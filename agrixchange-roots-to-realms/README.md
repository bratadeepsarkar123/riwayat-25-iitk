**Type:** Internal inter-hall problem statement (not a course project)

## Problem

Small and marginal farmers (85% of India's farming base) face credit gaps, middleman markups (25–55% of wholesale price), poor input quality, and no transparent link to capital. Middle-class contributors lack a safe, structured way to invest in agriculture.

## Proposed solution — AgriXchange

*"Your Investment, Their Harvest, Our Future"* — a farm-to-investor agritech platform that:

- Lets farmers offer a **share of upcoming harvest** with past profit history for informed co-funding
- **Manages funds directly** — procures seeds, equipment, and inputs from trusted suppliers instead of unstructured cash transfers
- Provides **vernacular mobile app** with video-based progress uploads every **10 days**, analyzed by AI for crop health and pricing compliance
- Connects **farmers, grocery/retail buyers, and investors** in one traceable ecosystem aligned with UN SDGs 1, 8, 12, 17

## Market sizing (from report)

- **TAM:** 125M+ small/m marginal farmer households; 460M+ middle-class Indians
- **Credit gap:** formal ag credit penetration ~20%; ₹20–25L Cr unmet demand (CRISIL 2024)
- **Digital adoption:** farmer digital payments 11% → 43% (2022–2024, McKinsey Global Farmers Insights)

## Tech stack (planned)

React.js / Node.js, AWS + GCP, TensorFlow/PyTorch for crop monitoring, integrated KYC and payments.
