## Problem

India's healthcare is largely reactive; ~60% of deaths from diabetes and heart disease. Hospitals, labs, and clinics operate in silos; paper reports are not digitized; patients repeat tests; early warning signs are missed.

## Proposed solution — AIVital

AI-powered **predictive healthcare platform** that:

- **Digitizes** reports and prescriptions via OCR + NLP into unified digital health records
- **Predicts** disease risk (diabetes, heart, kidney focus) before symptoms via multi-modal AI
- **Advises** doctors and patients with real-time risk alerts and next-step recommendations
- Connects **doctors, hospitals, labs, insurers, pharma, and patients** on one ecosystem
- **NDHM / HIPAA-compliant** privacy-first architecture; self-learning models improve with each report
